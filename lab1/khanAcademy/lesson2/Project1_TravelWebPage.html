Travel to China: Beijing

Beijing is the capital of the People's Republic of China
and a major global hub for politics, culture, education, and innovation.

Where to go in Beijing?

  • The Great Wall of China - one of the most famous landmarks in the world.
  • Forbidden City - а huge imperial palace from the Ming and Qing dynasties.
  • Temple of Heaven - a beautiful temple complex where emperors used to pray for good harvests.

All these places are wonderful to get culturally inspired by Chinese Culture!

What to eat in Beijing?

  1. Peking Duck (北京烤鸭) - The most famous dish in Beijing.
  2. Jianbing (煎饼) - a savory Chinese crepe often eaten for breakfast.
  3. Zhajiangmian (炸酱面) - Noodles with soybean paste sauce.
