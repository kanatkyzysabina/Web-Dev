Adventure Time

Posted on 25.01.2025

Adventure Time (2010–2018) is a surreal animated series by Pendleton Ward following
12-year-old Finn the Human and his shape-shifting dog brother, Jake, in the post-apocalyptic Land of Ooo. They navigate whimsical, often dark, adventures fighting monsters, rescuing princesses, and navigating complex emotional growth.

Avatar: The Last Airbender

Posted on 25.01.2025

Avatar: The Last Airbender (2005–2008) is a critically acclaimed animated series set in an Asian-inspired, war-torn world where certain people "bend" one of four elements: Water, Earth, Fire, or Air. The story follows 12-year-old Aang, the last Air Nomad and the Avatar, who must master all elements to stop the imperialistic Fire Nation.

Top 3 Best Episodes (in my opinion)

  1. 7th episode (season 2) - "Zuko Alone"
  2. 13th episode (season 3) - "The Firebending Masters"
  3. 15th episode (season 2) - "The Tales of Ba Sing Se"

Steven Universe

Posted on 25.01.2025

Steven Universe is a popular Cartoon Network animated series (2013–2019) created by Rebecca Sugar that follows a young, half-human/half-Gem boy named Steven who lives with the Crystal Gems—Garnet, Amethyst, and Pearl—in Beach City. He protects the world using inherited, emotion-based magical powers from his gemstone.

Top 3 Best Episodes (in my opinion)

  1. 18th episode (season 5) - "A Single Pale Rose"
  2. 52nd episode (season 1) - "Jail Break"
  3. 4th episode (season 4) - "Mindful Education"
