Recipe #2: Simple Vegetable Soup

  • Time: ~30 min
  • Serves: ~4
Ingredients Quantity
Onion Garlic Tomatoes Water Corn(canned) Vermicelli Oil Salt+pepper
40g 3 cloves 3 (270g) 100 ml 80g 50g 1 tbsp to your taste

Step 1: Chop pepper, onion, garlic; fry in oil 3 min.
Step 2: Add tomatoes + water; simmer ~20 min.
Step 3: Blend to smooth; return to heat.
Step 4: Add vermicelli + corn; cook 5 min.
Step 5: Season with salt & pepper.

Mixed lettuce greens Cucumber Carrot Olive oil Lemon juice
